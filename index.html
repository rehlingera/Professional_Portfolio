About Me

I work in mental health and am currently filling a position in a group home organization as a Quality Compliance Specialist. We specialize in providing services to individuals with a history of mental illness, traumatic brain injury and substance abuse. As part of my role I am working on a mobile app which allows our management employees greater mobility in their jobs. I am very attracted to the design aspect of the app. I am using Microsoft PowerApps Studio to develop it, as it is secure and connects to the entire Office 365 suite, which we use for almost all our work.
